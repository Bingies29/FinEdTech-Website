About Us

FinEdTech is a platform dedicated to teaching financial literacy and coding to the youth of Ghana. Our mission is to empower students with the essential skills they need to thrive in both the digital and financial worlds. We are committed to fostering a generation of well-informed, tech-savvy individuals who are prepared for future challenges and opportunities.

Our History

Founded in 2023 by Ransford Aboagye, FinEdTech began with a vision to bridge the gap between traditional education and the growing demand for digital and financial skills. Since then, we have expanded our offerings and reached over 1,000 students across Ghana. Our innovative approach has set us apart as a leader in youth education.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a leading educational platform that not only imparts knowledge but also inspires innovation and entrepreneurship among the youth. We aim to continuously evolve our programs to meet the needs of a rapidly changing world and make a lasting impact on the future of our students.

Achievements

In our short time, we have:

  • Reached over 1,000 students across Ghana.
  • Partnered with local organizations such as BUILD SCITECH.
  • Received the Ghana Youth Education Award for our innovative educational programs.
